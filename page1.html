Major characters

MAJOR CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Ibarra or Crisostomo 		Full Name:
Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin

Ethnicity:
Spanish Mestizo
  • Son of Don Rafael Ibarra,a Filipino businessman
  • Earnest and idealistic young man
  • Studied in Europe for 7 years
  • Maria Clara's fiancé

Maria Clara or Maria		 Full Name:
María Clara de los Santos y Alba

Ethnicity:
Spanish Mestiza
  • Daughter of Kapitan Tiago
  • Most beautiful and widely celebrated girl in San Diego, Laguna
  • Illegitimate daughter of Father Dámaso
  • Ibarra's fiancé

Kapitan Tiago		 Full Name:
Santiago de los Santos

Ethnicity:
Indio
  • Only son of a sugar miller in Malabon
  • Richest man in the region of Binondo
  • Good Catholic and friend of the Spanish Government
  • Married to Doña Pia Alba

Padre Damaso		 Full Name:
Dámaso Verdolagas

Ethnicity:
Spanish
  • Franciscan friar and the former parish curate of San Diego
  • Enemy of Crisóstomo's father Don Rafael Ibarra
  • María Clara's biological father

Other characters

OTHER CHARACTERS IN NOLI ME TANGERE

Elias 		Full Name:
Unknown

Ethnicity:
Indio
  • Ibarra's mysterious friend and ally
  • Fugitive living in San Diego
  • Dedicated to helping the oppressed by society.
  • Close friend and sweetheart of Salome

Doña Victorina		 Full Name:
Doña Victorina de los Reyes de de Espadaña

Ethnicity:
Indio
  • Ambitious Filipina who classifies herself as a Spaniard
  • Married to Don Tiburcio de Espadaña, an official of the customs bureau
  • A socialite and friend of Capitan Tiago

Sisa		 Full Name:
Narcisa

Ethnicity:
Indio
  • Deranged mother of Basilio and Crispín living in San Diego
  • Caring but submissive woman
  • Maltreated by the alferez' wife Doña Consolacion
  • Died and buried near the tomb of Ibarra's great-grandfather

Basilio and Crispin		 Full Name:
Unknown

Ethnicity:
Indio
  • 2 young boys of Sisa who serve as sacristan in San Diego parish
  • Basilio was an optimistic and hopeful individual while Crispin was more of a clever boy who lacks self esteem
  • Accused of stealing two gold pieces by the sacristan mayor
